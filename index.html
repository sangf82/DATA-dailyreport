Privacy Policy for DiveVin Swimming Club at VinUniversity

Introduction

Welcome to DiveVin Swimming Club, a student organization at VinUniversity dedicated to promoting fitness, swimming skills, and camaraderie among students. By joining our club, you agree to the terms outlined in this privacy policy and code of conduct.

Code of Conduct

  • Mutual Respect: All members must treat each other with respect and kindness. We promote a friendly and inclusive environment where everyone is encouraged to participate and grow.
  • Active Participation: Members are expected to be active participants. If a member does not engage in club activities for more than one week, they will be removed from the club to maintain an active and engaged community.

Data Collection

We collect the following information to maintain our records:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and student ID for communication and activity registration purposes.
  • Activity Participation: We track attendance at swimming sessions and club events.

Data Usage

We use the collected data to:

  • Communicate with members about club activities and updates.
  • Monitor participation to ensure engagement with the club.

Data Protection

We take the necessary steps to protect your personal information and ensure it is used only for club-related activities. We do not share your information with third parties without your consent.

Member Rights

Members have the right to:

  • Access their personal data held by the club.
  • Request deletion of personal data when no longer part of the club.

Contact Information

For any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact the DiveVin Swimming Club at contact@divevin-swimming.com.
